Kambo

Amazonian Frog Medicine

What is Kambo?

Kambo is a powerful natural medicine derived from the secretion of the Giant Monkey Frog (Phyllomedusa bicolor), native to the Amazon rainforest. This sacred medicine has been used for centuries by indigenous tribes for physical and spiritual cleansing.

The secretion contains powerful bioactive peptides that stimulate the body's natural healing systems, providing deep physical, emotional, and energetic purification.

Kambo is known as the "vaccine of the forest" due to its powerful immune-boosting and detoxifying properties.

What to Expect in Ceremony

Preparation

Before receiving Kambo, participants drink 1-2 liters of water to support the purging process. It's important to come on an empty stomach (fasting for at least 8 hours before the ceremony).

During Ceremony

The Kambo is applied to small burns on the skin (called "gates"). The effects come on quickly, typically within minutes:

  • Increased heart rate and body temperature
  • Flushing and tingling sensations
  • Nausea and purging (vomiting)
  • Feeling of pressure or swelling
  • Deep physical and energetic release

The intense phase lasts 15-40 minutes, followed by a deep sense of calm, clarity, and renewal. The entire ceremony typically lasts 1-2 hours.

After Ceremony

Following the ceremony, participants often experience increased energy, mental clarity, and a sense of lightness. The benefits continue to unfold over the following days and weeks.
