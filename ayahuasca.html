What is Ayahuasca?

Ayahuasca is a sacred plant medicine that has been used for thousands of years by indigenous tribes of the Amazon rainforest for healing, spiritual growth, and connection with the divine.

The brew is traditionally made from the Banisteriopsis caapi vine and the Psychotria viridis leaf, creating a powerful entheogenic medicine that facilitates deep introspection and healing.

The name "Ayahuasca" comes from the Quechua words "aya" (spirit/soul) and "huasca" (vine/rope), literally meaning "vine of the soul" or "rope of the dead."

  • Release of trauma and past wounds
  • Spiritual awakening and connection
  • Increased self-awareness and clarity
  • Connection with ancestral wisdom
  • Profound insights into life purpose
  • Healing of depression and anxiety
  • Breaking addictive patterns

What to Expect in Ceremony

Preparation

Before the ceremony, participants follow a special diet (dieta) to prepare the body and mind. This includes avoiding certain foods, alcohol, and medications that could interfere with the medicine.

During Ceremony

The ceremony takes place in the evening in a safe, sacred space. After drinking the medicine, participants lie down and journey inward. The experience typically lasts 4-6 hours and can include:

  • Visions and geometric patterns
  • Deep emotional releases (purging)
  • Insights and revelations
  • Connection with the divine
  • Healing on physical, emotional, and spiritual levels

Integration

The days and weeks following ceremony are crucial for integration. Our facilitators provide ongoing support to help you understand and integrate your experience into daily life.

Our Approach

We honor the traditional indigenous practices while providing a safe, supportive environment for Western participants. Our ceremonies are led by experienced facilitators who have studied extensively with indigenous shamans.

Each ceremony includes:

  • Traditional icaros (healing songs)
  • Safe, comfortable setting
  • Experienced facilitators
  • Individual attention and support
  • Pre and post-ceremony integration

Is Ayahuasca Right for You?

Ayahuasca is a powerful medicine that requires respect, preparation, and the right mindset. It's ideal for those seeking:

  • Deep healing and transformation
  • Spiritual growth and awakening
  • Release from trauma or addiction
  • Life direction and clarity
  • Connection with something greater

Please note: Ayahuasca is not recommended for those with certain medical conditions or taking specific medications. A thorough health screening is required before participating.
