What is Qi Gong?

Qi Gong (pronounced "chee-gong") is an ancient Chinese practice that combines movement, breath, and meditation to cultivate and balance life force energy (Qi or Chi) within the body.

With roots dating back over 4,000 years, Qi Gong has been used for healing, spiritual development, and longevity. The practice integrates slow, flowing movements with focused breathing and meditative awareness.

The name "Qi Gong" translates to "energy work" or "cultivating energy," reflecting its purpose of harmonizing the body's vital energy for optimal health and spiritual growth.

  • Enhanced immune system function

  • Mental clarity and focus
  • Emotional balance and stability

Is Qi Gong Right for You?

Qi Gong is suitable for everyone, regardless of age or fitness level. It's particularly beneficial for:

  • Beginners to energy practices
  • Those seeking gentle movement
  • People recovering from illness or injury
  • Anyone wanting to reduce stress
  • Those preparing for or integrating plant medicine
  • Anyone seeking mind-body-spirit connection
  • Those wanting sustainable daily practice

No experience necessary: Our practices are designed to be accessible and beneficial for complete beginners while offering depth for experienced practitioners.
