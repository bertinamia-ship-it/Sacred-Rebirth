About Michelle & Brian

Your Retreat Facilitators

Michelle and Brian are experienced facilitators dedicated to the path of healing and spiritual growth. With years of training in plant medicine ceremonies and traditional healing practices, they create a safe and sacred space for transformation.

Together, they guide participants through profound journeys of self-discovery, healing, and awakening, honoring sacred traditions while holding space with love and compassion.
